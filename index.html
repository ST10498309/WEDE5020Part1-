Shani's Bakes

Welcome to Shani's Bakes. Every taste should feel like home to us at Shani's Bakes. Made with love, attention, and only the best ingredients, our delicacies range from soft, buttery pastries to freshly baked bread and rich cakes. We have something just for you, whether you're searching for a warm loaf of bread to share with family, a batch of cookies to share with friends, or the ideal cake to commemorate a special occasion.

  • Freshly baked every day.
  • Made with high-quality ingredients and love.
  • Ideal for any situation.

What do we do?

At Shani's Bakes, we are more than just a bakery we are a cozy haven where everything is made with love. Our focus is on small-batch baking, personal touches, and making every customer feel welcome. Personalised Cakes, Custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, or any occasion, crafted to match your style and taste.

Freshly Baked Goods, Warm bread, buttery croissants, and pastries baked fresh every morning. Sweet Treats Cookies, brownies, and cupcakes are perfect for a snack, gift, or little indulgence. Coffee & Comfort Enjoy your favourite treat with a fresh cup of tea or coffee in our cozy space. At Shani's Bakes, we believe every occasion deserves something sweet and every day deserves a little happiness.

Why choose Us?

At Shani's Bakes, we think that the smallest details have the greatest impact. We use only the best ingredients and bake everything fresh in small amounts to guarantee the best flavor. From exquisitely personalized cakes to welcoming, amiable service that elevates every visit, we personalize every order. Our bakery is more than simply a store; it's a warm, inviting area where you can unwind, indulge, and make happy memories.
